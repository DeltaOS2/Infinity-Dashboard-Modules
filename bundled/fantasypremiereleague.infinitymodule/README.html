Finding your Player ID

  • Click the following link to log into Fantasy Premiere League
  • Follow the on screen steps to log in
  • Once you’re logged in, click the Points tab
  • Your Player ID will be displayed in the URL bar of your browser
  • Your Player ID is listed after the team/ text and ends before the next /
    • An example Player ID is: 1234567
https://fantasy.premierleague.com/a/team/<YOUR_USER_ID>/event/...

Entering your Player ID into Infinity Dashboard

  • Open Infinity Dashboard
  • Click on the ‘Module Config’ item in the sidebar
  • Locate the Fantasy Premier League Points module you want to configure
  • Click the Pencil icon to open the module configuration
  • Locate the Player ID config option
  • Click the Your fantasy.premierleague.com Player ID text field to edit the value

Success — You’re all set!
